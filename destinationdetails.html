Maasai Mara National Researve

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the Maasai Mara, Kenya's most famous wildlife conservation area. Renowned for its abundant wildlife, including the 'Big Five', and as the stage for the Great Migration, the Mara offers unparalleled safari experiences, vast savannas, and rich cultural encounters with the Maasai people."

Short yet immersive safari focusing on game drives.

Itineraries

3 Days Mara Explorer Safari $400

  • Day 1: Arrival in Maasai Mara & Afternoon Game Drive
    • Morning flight from Nairobi to Maasai Mara.
    • Transfer to camp/lodge.
    • Lunch and relaxation.
    • Afternoon game drive until sunset.
  • Day 2: Full Day Safari Exploration
    • Early morning game drive, ideal for predator sightings.
    • Bush breakfast (optional).
    • Mid-day leisure or optional cultural visit to a Maasai village.
    • Afternoon game drive.
  • Day 3: Departure
    • Morning game drive (optional).
    • Breakfast at camp.
    • Transfer to airstrip for flight back to Nairobi.
