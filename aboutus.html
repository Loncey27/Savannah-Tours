About Savannah-Travels

Our Story

Welcome to Savannah-Travels, your dedicated partner in crafting unforgettable African adventures. Founded with a deep love for the continent's breathtaking landscapes and vibrant cultures, we specialize in curating bespoke safari, beach, and cultural experiences across East Africa's most iconic destinations. From the sweeping plains of the Maasai Mara to the pristine shores of Zanzibar and the majestic peaks of Kilimanjaro, we are committed to transforming your travel dreams into cherished realities.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

Mission:To create authentic, sustainable, and transformative travel experiences in Africa, connecting our guests with the continent's unparalleled natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage, while contributing positively to local communities and conservation efforts.

Our Vision

Vision: To be the leading and most trusted luxury travel outfitter for African safaris and bespoke journeys, recognized for our exceptional service, deep local expertise, and unwavering commitment to responsible tourism.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Delivering unparalleled service and meticulously planned itineraries.
  • Authenticity: Providing genuine, immersive experiences that connect travelers with local cultures and nature.
  • Sustainability: Championing ethical tourism practices that protect wildlife and benefit communities.
  • Passion: Our team's genuine love for Africa drives every itinerary we create.
  • Personalization: Tailoring every journey to the unique desires and interests of our guests.

Our Journey

  • 2020: Savannah-Travels is founded with a vision to offer personalized safari experiences, initially focusing on Kenya and Tanzania.
  • 2021: Expanded operations to include Uganda, introducing gorilla trekking and unique cultural tours.
  • 2022: Launched our first online booking platform, streamlining the planning process for international clients.
  • 2023: Celebrated serving over 5,000 happy travelers, expanding our network of trusted local partners and guides.
  • 2024: Introduced new adventure packages, including mountain climbing expeditions and marine conservation tours.
  • 2025: Recognized as a top-tier operator for sustainable tourism in East Africa, continuing to innovate and expand our offerings.
